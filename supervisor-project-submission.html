How to Submit a BTR Project

If you have a research project opportunity for MSP Bachelor students, you can submit it to the BTR Office. Projects are shared with students through Canvas and the BTR Advertisement Board.

What Happens Next?

After you submit your project:

  • The BTR Office will review your submission
  • We may contact you if we have any questions
  • Approved projects are posted on Canvas and the BTR Advertisement Board (1st floor, opposite the lifts)
  • Students who are interested in your project will contact you directly

Physical Advertisement Board

The BTR Advertisement Board is located on the first floor of Paul-Henri Spaaklaan 1 (PHS1), opposite the lifts. Students can view project descriptions from MSP staff members and external researchers.
