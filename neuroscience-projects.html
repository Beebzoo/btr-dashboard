Biopsychology, Cognitive & Affective Neuroscience

Research focuses on the psychobiology of stress, emotions and decision making in humans. Projects include decision making and heart rate variability, decision making and interoception and alexithymia, and stress and decision making.

Dr. Christian Herff Maastricht Computational Neuroscience

Brain State Decoding using Machine Learning

Decode cognitive processes such as memory, speech, or decision making from invasive brain recordings using machine learning algorithms. Work with high-quality neural data from direct brain measurements.

Dr. Lars Hausfeld Maastricht Psychoacoustics/Cognitive Neuroscience

Auditory Processing and Speech-in-Noise

Study how the brain processes speech in noisy environments, investigating the 'cocktail party problem' through psychoacoustics and neuroimaging techniques including EEG and fMRI.
