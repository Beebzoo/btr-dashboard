Welcome to the BTR Dashboard

The Bachelor Thesis Research (BTR) is the final and most significant component of your Maastricht Science Programme journey. This 30 ECTS trajectory spans 5 months and represents your transition from student to independent researcher.

What is the BTR?

The BTR is a comprehensive research project where you'll conduct original scientific research under the guidance of experienced supervisors. You'll develop a research question, design and execute experiments, analyze data, and communicate your findings through both written and video formats.

Why This Dashboard?

This centralized platform brings together all essential BTR information - from eligibility requirements and deadlines to writing guidelines and assessment rubrics. Whether you're just starting to plan your BTR or deep in the writing process, you'll find the resources you need here.
